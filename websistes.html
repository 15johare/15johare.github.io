TESLA'S INFOTAINMENT

Tesla was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who wanted to prove that people didn’t need to compromise to drive electric.

That electric vehicles can be better, quicker and more fun to drive than gasoline cars. Today, Tesla builds not only all-electric vehicles but also infinitely scalable clean energy generation and storage products.

Tesla believes the faster the world stops relying on fossil fuels and moves towards a zero-emission future, the better.

ELON MUSK

Elon Reeve Musk was born on June 28th 1971 in South Africa. Musk is the founder, CEO, and CTO of Space-X, and a co-founder, CEO and Product Architect at Tesla, overseeing all product development, engineering, and design of the company's electric vehicles, battery products, and solar roofs. Since the company’s inception in 2003, Tesla’s mission has been to accelerate the transition to a sustainable energy future., and is the 80th richest person in the world.
